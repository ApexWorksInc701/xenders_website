Vision

The vision of this organisation is to create a permanent platform to raise inspirational and change makers for total community transformation.

Mission

This organisation exists to build sustainable communities through raising of dynamic and transformational leaders for entrepreneurship and employable skills training, competitive agribusinesses, decentralisation and good governance, infrastructural development for quality education and ICT education for innovative human capital training.

Objectives

Short-Term Objectives

  • To promote leadership training in order to produce young inspirational leaders.
  • To promote entrepreneurship, employable skills and agribusiness.
  • To assist local communities with building materials to construct basic school infrastructures including sanitation facilities as well as teachers' quarters to attract quality teachers into the District.
  • To organise Public-Private-Partnership (PPP) dialogue among stakeholders who include: Traditional Authority, District Assembly, Farmer Based Organisation (FBOs), Business and Entrepreneurial organisations and deepen and promote decentralisation and good governance for sustainable territorial development.

Long-Term Objectives

  • To periodically conduct research into education in order to come out with creative solutions for emerging challenges confronting teaching and learning in that district.
  • To carry out welfare activities and development works of any nature that will enhance the quality of life of both children and the old.
  • To promote the health status of the people by organising periodic health screening exercises in deprived communities within the operational area.
  • To establish partnerships with local authorities, NGOs, Volunteer-Organisations and charities supporting the course of children and young people, working in collaboration with these agencies to improve lifestyles and development.
  • To establish a professional ICT Training Centre to provide well focused training in the Software and Hardware aspects of a Computer (ICT in general).
