Core Activities / Projects

Below are the core activites or project organised by the foundations:

  • Donations
  • Volunteer Work
  • Vocational Training
  • Motivational Speeches

Donations

We make donations to orphanges, hospitals, prisons, widows. We also help people with exceptionally health challenges who are financially handicapped.

Volunteer Works

We organise or accept volunteer works such as Clean Up Exercises, Vacation classes, Health Talks, Girl Child Education Campaigns.

Vocational Training

Forums are organised to train the youths to develop skills in agribusiness, rearing of non-traditional animals, processing of farm products etc.

Motivational Speeches

We organise programs to challenge and encourage the youths on how to achieve the dreams in life. We also educate the youth about entrepreneurship.
