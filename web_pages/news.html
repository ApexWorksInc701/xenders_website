Time with Widows and Orphans of Sefwi Nsounsua Wiawso Muncipal

Xenders Charity Foundation in collaboration with Matcom Company Limited in their charitable quest lavished the widows and orphans of Sefwi Nsuonsua Wiawso Muncipal with a lot of items which made the widows and orphans very honoured and happy this Christmas. The items donated included: nine(9) bags of rice, four(4) boxes of oil (one litre each), forty(40) pieces of Bedsheets, Washing powders, two(2) boxes of Sardine, two(2) boxes of toothbrush, three(3) packs of exercise books, three(3) boxes of pen and three(3) boxes of Tuna. A total number of seventy (70) orphans and one hundred and five (105) windows benefited from this donation. The chief of Wiawso Traditional Council, Nana Elluo Panyin, Sefwi Nsuosua Kyidomhene was present as the special guest of honour to grace the occasion. The donation was spearheaded by Alexander Adjei, CEO of the foundation. The foundation expresses her heart felt gratitude to all and sundry who assisted in various ways to put smiles on the faces of these widows and orphans and making their Christmas memorable.

Posted on Tuesday, 21 January, 2020; 12:30 pm.
